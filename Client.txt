KPI's Requirement:
KPI: Key Performance Indicator, gives us overall idea of business, gives aggregation of values at high level.
We need to analyze key indicators for our pizza sales data to gain insights
performance. Specifically, we want to calculate the following metrics:
Measures: Total Revenue, Average Order Value, Total Pizzas Sold, Total Orders, Average Pizzas Per Order

Charts Requirement:
1. Daily Trend for Total Orders: Bar chart that displays daily trend over a period of time.
2. Monhtly trend for total orders: line chart for it to understand the pattern.
3. Percentage of sales by pizza category: Pie chart. 
4. Percentage of sales by pizza size: Pie chart
5. Total Pizza sold by pizza category: Create a funnel chart that presents the total number of pizzas sold for each pizza category. This chart will allow us to compare the sales performance of different pizza categories.
6. Top 5 Best Sellers by Revenue, Total Quantity, and Total Orders: Bar Chart
7. Bottom 5 Sellers by Revenue, Total Quantity, and Total Orders: Bar Chart
